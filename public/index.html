Methodology

Candles are real: each monthly bar's open/high/low/close is the TRAX index evaluated at every provider repricing date within the month, and volume is the count of those repricing events.

Disclaimer

TRAX is an analytics tool, not financial advice or a tradable instrument. There is no token futures market — list prices change on announcement, not by the second. The LIVE readout simulates microstructure around the real current index level for presentation only. Prices are curated from public provider announcements (per million tokens, USD); edit lib/trax.js to refine.
