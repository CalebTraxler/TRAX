Data & disclaimer

Prices are curated from public provider announcements (per million tokens, USD) and are approximate — edit lib/trax.js to refine. The LIVE readout simulates intraday microstructure around the real current index level; underlying data updates when providers change list prices, not by the second. TRAX is an analytics tool, not financial advice or a tradable instrument.
